The default iOS keypad is not very convenient for numeric input. Using <input type="number"> makes it a little better, but there are still too many extra keys taking up space. pattern="[0-9]*" gives a nice numpad, but it lacks symbols, and makes some browsers perform validation on the field. So, I made my own keypad! It has everything needed for calculating board footage, and nothing more.
